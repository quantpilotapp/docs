## Token Supply & Distribution

We begin with a fixed supply of 1,000,000,000 QuantPilot's tokens, allocated as follows:

Aligned incentives for core contributors who drive QuantPilot's development and adoption.

### Investors (x%)

### Treasury (x%)

Used for grants, community initiatives, liquidity expansion, and overall ecosystem growth.

### Liquidity Pool (x%)

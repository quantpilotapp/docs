Welcome to the introduction section of QuantPilot.

QuantPilot is an AI oracle with a proprietary data lake and AI models powering the decision making of agents and humans.

This section is dedicated to providing an overview of the project and its goals. QuantPilot was created by a group of developers and researchers who are passionate about the power of AI agents.

After reading this section, you will gain a clearer understanding of the project's origin, mission, team, and the problem we aim to solve.
